    How To Play

  • Use the arrow keys to move your player
  • Reach the water to earn three points
  • Collect gems to earn one point
  • Hitting a rock loses a point
  • Hitting a bug loses a life
  • Having negative points loses the game
  • Earn ten points to win the game
